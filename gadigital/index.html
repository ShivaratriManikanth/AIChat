Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding skills to setup the bot? +
Not at all! Our platform is 100% no-code. You can train the AI by simply uploading a PDF or entering your website URL, and embed it using a simple script tag we provide.
Can I switch between AI and Manual Flows? +
Yes. You can use our drag-and-drop Flow Builder to design specific conversation paths (like appointment booking), and let the AI handle general questions.
